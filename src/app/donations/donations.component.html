100% Transparent

Where Does the Money Go?

The CSE DIU Alumni Association is committed to full financial transparency. Every taka collected is accounted for, reported to the general members, and independently verified. No administrative deductions are taken from designated campaign funds.

  • 100% of campaign donations go directly to the stated purpose
  • Financial reports published quarterly on the alumni portal
  • All campaigns approved by the elected committee before launch
  • Donor receipts and tax documentation provided on request
  • Surplus funds require member vote before reallocation

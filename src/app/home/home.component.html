About the network

Built by graduates,
for graduates

The CSE DIU Alumni Network was founded to bridge the gap between past and present students of Dhaka International University's CSE department. We believe that the relationships built during university years should last a lifetime.

Whether you graduated last year or a decade ago, this is your home — a place to find old friends, discover new opportunities, and contribute to the next generation of engineers.
